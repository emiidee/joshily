Our Story

Hello

Emily and Josh met in college and quickly became best friends. Boba was a key ingredient in their budding friendship, and it’s become a central theme in their lives as a couple.

Let's go out

Josh tried to ask Emily out for years, but it wasn’t until late 2010 that Emily finally said “yes”. Their first two years were spent in LA as Emily finished college and Josh worked at a startup.

Parenting sounds like a GOOD idea

Bowser joined the family in 2011 after he was the last puppy in his litter to be picked. It was lucky for his humans, since he was the puppy they had picked all along! Emily and Josh got Apple in Manhattan later that year. They fell in love with her at first sight and flew back home with her that day!

San Francisco

The whole family moved back to San Francisco in 2012, where they live now.

Will you marry me?

The Proposal

With the help of friends old and new, Josh convinced Emily to fly out to LA. He took her to a rooftop helipad downtown under the guise of location scouting for a work photoshoot. Josh barely managed, completely forgetting English and bumbling around with the ring, but in the end, he proposed on the rooftop overlooking the city where they had met. Despite all of that, Emily said yes!

Friends had flown in from all over the country to celebrate, and they surprised Emily at lunch. It was a terrific start to the engagement!
